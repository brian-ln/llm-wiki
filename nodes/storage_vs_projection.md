# Storage vs. Projection (The CQRS of Thought)

**Context:** The storage of knowledge can and must be separate from its projection. A relational database is not the information it stores; it is merely a search index. 

The underlying truth (the event stream/ledger) can have infinite, simultaneous, imperfect projections. This concept bridges the gap between **immutable algorithmic memory** (the ledger, storing what actually happened or was stated) and **fluid, reconstructive human-like recall** (the materialized views, adapting information for immediate contextual use). 

As the adage goes: *"All models are wrong, some are useful."*
